Account Deletion

Delete your RoamTrip account

You can request deletion of your RoamTrip account and all associated data at any time.

1 Open the RoamTrip app

Make sure you're signed in to the account you want to delete.

2 Go to your Profile

Tap the Profile tab in the bottom navigation bar.

3 Open Settings

Scroll to the bottom of your profile and tap Settings.

4 Delete account

Tap Delete Account and confirm. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Can't access the app?

Email us

Send a deletion request from the email address associated with your account to info@roamtrip.mobile. We will process your request within 30 days.

What happens to your data

Data type On deletion
Profile (name, email, avatar) Permanently deleted immediately
Trips, events, itineraries Permanently deleted immediately
Journal entries Permanently deleted immediately
Photos and gallery Permanently deleted immediately
Trip Books (PDFs) Permanently deleted immediately
Vault data Permanently deleted immediately
Subscription records Retained for up to 7 years as required by financial regulations
Anonymised usage logs Retained for up to 90 days then permanently deleted
