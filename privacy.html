Privacy Policy

Effective date: 26 May 2026  ·  Last updated: 26 May 2026

RoamTrip ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it when you use the RoamTrip mobile application ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

Account Information
When you create a RoamTrip account, we collect your email address and, optionally, your first name. This information is required to provide the service.

Trip and Itinerary Data
Information you enter into the App — including destinations, travel dates, flight details, accommodation, events, transport, and budget — is stored to provide the core trip management service.

Location Data
With your permission, the App accesses your device location while in use to provide location-aware features including live safety scores, weather intelligence, golden hour timing, and nearby points of interest. We do not track your location in the background or when the App is closed.

Photos and Camera
With your permission, the App accesses your camera and photo library for VibeFrame photo capture and to attach photos to trips and journal entries. Photos are stored in your account and are not used for any other purpose.

Documents
Documents you store in the RoamTrip Vault (passports, visas, insurance) are encrypted on your device and stored securely in your account. These documents are never shared or used for any purpose other than displaying them to you within the App.

Financial Data
Expense records you enter into the spend tracker are stored in your account. We do not collect, access, or store any banking or payment card information.

Device Information
We may collect basic device information (device type, operating system version, app version) for the purpose of crash reporting and improving app performance.

Push Notification Tokens
If you opt in to notifications, we store a push notification token associated with your account to deliver alerts (safety, flight, weather, events).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To provide trip management, intelligence alerts, and itinerary features
  • To send push notifications you have opted in to receive
  • To deliver the RT-Smart daily morning briefing during active trips
  • To process in-app subscription purchases (via RevenueCat)
  • To improve the App through anonymised usage analytics and crash reporting
  • To respond to support enquiries

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services to deliver its features. Each service has its own privacy policy.

  • Supabase — Secure cloud database and authentication. Your account data and trip information is stored on Supabase infrastructure.
  • Google Maps & Google Places — Mapping, route display, and location search.
  • HERE Technologies — Points of interest data.
  • AeroDataBox — Live flight status and schedule data.
  • OpenWeatherMap — Weather intelligence and forecast data.
  • Unsplash — Destination photography used for trip visuals.
  • Firebase (Google) — Push notification delivery.
  • RevenueCat — In-app subscription management. RevenueCat may collect purchase history in accordance with their privacy policy.
  • MapTiler — Satellite and terrain map tiles.

We only share the minimum data necessary with each third-party service to deliver the relevant feature. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party.

4. Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encrypted connections. Vault documents are protected by device biometric authentication (Face ID / fingerprint) and are encrypted at rest. We follow industry-standard practices to protect your information.

While we take reasonable precautions, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and to keep your device software up to date.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data and trip history will be permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it for longer.

6. Children's Privacy

RoamTrip is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Object to or restrict certain uses of your data
  • Data portability (receive a copy of your data in a common format)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@roamtrip.mobile.

8. Cookies and Tracking

The RoamTrip mobile app does not use cookies. We may use anonymised, aggregated analytics to understand how the App is used, but this data cannot be used to identify you individually.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via the App or by email before the changes take effect. The effective date at the top of this page will always reflect the latest revision.
